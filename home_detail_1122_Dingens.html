About the Home

Refined Interiors

Soaring ceilings and engineered hardwood floors create a bright, welcoming atmosphere throughout. A grand foyer greets guests on arrival, opening to a majestic living room with shiplap ceiling detail and an open-concept layout that connects living, dining, and kitchen — designed for effortless everyday living and elevated entertaining. Eight-foot interior and exterior doors, custom millwork with 5½" baseboards, recessed LED lighting, and spacious walk-in closets carry through the home.

Gourmet Kitchen

At the center of the home, the chef-inspired kitchen pairs a premium appliance package and gas range with sleek solid-surface countertops and an oversized island built for gatherings. A walk-in butler’s pantry and custom cabinetry — 42" uppers, soft-close hardware, and under-cabinet lighting — complete the space.

Dual Primary Suites

Two luxurious primary suites, one on each floor, make the home ideal for multigenerational living or private guest accommodations. Each features a spa-style bath with designer tile and solid-surface countertops; the main suite adds a freestanding soaking tub and a spa-like walk-in shower.

Outdoor Living

The private backyard retreat is built for year-round enjoyment, with a sparkling pool, covered lanai, and full summer kitchen set on professionally landscaped, oversized grounds offering privacy and serenity. A porte-cochère provides covered parking, and a welcoming front porch stretches more than 22 feet.
